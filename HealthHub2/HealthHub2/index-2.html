MRI

Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) is a non-invasive medical imaging technique that uses powerful magnets and radio waves to produce detailed images of the body's internal structures.

X-ray

X-ray is a pioneering medical imaging technique that uses ionizing radiation to capture images of the body's internal structures. It is commonly used to diagnose bone fractures.
